What happens after I sign up?

Your coach sets up your profile and sends your app PIN within 24 hours. You'll complete your onboarding assessment and be ready to log your first session same day.

No lock-in. Every plan is month-to-month. We'd rather earn your commitment through results than a contract.

Can I upgrade tiers later?

Yes — at any time. Your program, logs, and progress carry over. You just unlock more of your coach's time and attention.

Do I need to download an app?

No App Store needed. CrazyyFit installs directly from your browser on iPhone, Android, or desktop.
